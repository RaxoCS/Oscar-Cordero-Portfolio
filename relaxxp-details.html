Relax XP Details

What, How and Why?

  • Unity VR & Mobile
  • 09/23/2022

Day by day people deal with dangerous situations, causing the brain to enter a state of alert inducing symptoms such as: nervousness, tremor, hyperventilation, lack of concentration, among others. Making people unable to continue with their daily activities no matter how easy they may seem to them. Thanks to this, applications have been created to try to deal with this problem, however, the charge requested from the user exceeds the budget limit and/or does not meet the user's quality standards. With this in mind, the "Relax XP" application arises, which aims to mitigate the problem that stress represents for the average user, implementing striking technologies such as virtual reality, making it accessible to the user thanks to its development for mobile devices, in turn, counting on its relevant research with professionals in the area of psychology to treat stress in a more effective and professional way.

This application, which was made with the purpose of participating in contests of technological projects such as EXPOCIENCIAS, was designed in Unity, with the help of Google Cardboard technology, with this we could focus the application to the target audience we were looking for, which were people who may not have the economic stability to enjoy virtual reality experiences with vr sets like Oculus, but enough to buy a vr headset for the cell phone that did not exceed $400 mx ($25 dls).

The artistic style was decided with the help of professionals in the field of psychology, in meetings with them, we came to the conclusion that one of the techniques for people to reach a state of calm, is to connect with their "child self". This responds to what a child would most likely call his attention, among those things that could call his attention are styles such as the chosen one, which is Low Poly, this also helps people not to be even more overwhelmed by the situation they are living, by not having to process so much information to see their environment.

My role in the project

My main roles in this project were:

  • Project Manager: I assumed the responsibility of preparing the documentation required for the contest in which I participated. In addition, I coordinated the agendas and drafted minutes with both the team members and the professionals with whom we collaborated. These steps were taken to provide the application with the necessary professional integrity and to ensure that it met its objectives effectively.

  • Game Programmer: My fundamental responsibility included the development of the game logic, addressing crucial aspects such as the user's movement control, the transition between the different scenes, the implementation of the music, and the creation of elements that would enhance the player's experience, such as the water that is seen in the game. My work also included the task of testing and verifying that the game worked correctly, meeting the expected performance and gameplay standards, and ensuring that it met the expectations of the application's users.

  • 3D Environmental Artist: My main function was to import and assemble the models designed by the 3D modeler, in order to create an environment that harmonized perfectly with the aesthetics we were pursuing in the project. This task, in particular, was the subject of extensive consultations with the psychologists with whom we collaborated, as it was a critical component for the achievement of the game's main objective. Ensuring cohesion and the right atmosphere in the environment was essential to the success of the project as a whole.
